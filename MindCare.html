About Us

MindCare AI is a pioneering initiative developed at Manipal University Jaipur, dedicated to revolutionizing mental health care through technology. Our team combines expertise in computer science and mental health to create innovative solutions.

We believe in making mental health support accessible to everyone, regardless of location or financial status. Our platform bridges the gap between patients and professionals through cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Mission

Accessible Care

To make quality mental health support available to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Innovation

To continuously innovate and improve our platform using the latest AI technologies.

Community

To build a supportive community that reduces stigma around mental health.

Key Features

AI-Powered Chatbots

Offering 24/7 support and evidence-based interventions with our advanced AI chatbot technology.

Real-Time Teletherapy

Facilitating live sessions with licensed mental health professionals for personalized care.

Doctor Onboarding

Streamlined onboarding process for licensed professionals to expand our network of experts.
